About Arovan

We design and craft furniture that grows with youmeant to be lived with, loved, and passed down. Each piece is a blend of timeless design and enduring quality, made to move with your life from one space to the next, generation after generation. For us, its not just about furnitureits about creating meaningful, lasting beauty for your home.

Meet The Founder

Where Craft Becomes Legacy

Founded in [Year], Arovan was born from a simple idea to transform natural wood into functional art. What started as a small workshop driven by creativity has evolved into a furniture studio known for its bold, expressive epoxy pieces.

We believe that furniture should tell a storyof the tree it came from, the hands that shaped it, and the home it becomes a part of. Every slab of wood we use is handpicked for its character, and every table we craft is built with intention.
